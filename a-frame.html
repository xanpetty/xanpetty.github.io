The font works perfectly with A-frame however it isn't 3D and the letters disappear when the front surface isn't facing the viewer.
There are more efficient ways to display 3D text but as a proof of concept I used Blender to convert an SVG of the letter 'a' from my Arial-Black-Hollow font into a 3D model that can be dragged by a mouse.
a.gltf model binary data for model
The aframe-text-geometry-component makes it possible to use 3D text with A-frame.
